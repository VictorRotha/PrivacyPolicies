# Privacy Policy for MPlayerRemote


## Advertising: 

This app uses AdMob from Google as ad provider. AdMob may collect data and uses an unique identifier to show relevant ads for you. 

In compliance with the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation), users in the EEA (European Economic Area) will see a message asking for consent. You can always change your consent options via the settings screen.

In compliance with the CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act), users in protected US states can opt out of data sharing via the settings screen.


## In App Purchases

This app uses the billing service from Google to process in app purchases. 

When you purchase an in-app item, that transaction is processed by Google Play. We can see only the very limited view of the transaction, like an order ID generated by the app store, price information, the date and time of the purchase, and sometimes the ZIP code of the purchaser. We only use this data to request refunds. We do not share these data with a third party.

Please see the privacy policies for Google here: https://policies.google.com/privacy and here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about the practices, please contact the Service Provider via email at victor.rotha.develope@gmail.com.
